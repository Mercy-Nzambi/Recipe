Recipe For A Tiramisu Cake

Ingredients

  1. 1 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  2. 1 Cup of mascarpone Cheese(That is at Room Temperature)
  3. 1/3 Cup of Sugar
  4. 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  5. 1 Cup of Strong Coffee or Expresso
  6. 1 pack of LadyFingers
  7. Cacao Powder for dusting

What To Do

  1. Beat whipping cream, sugar and vanilla until soft peaks form.
  2. Add in cheese and combine until stiff peaks form.
  3. Quickly dip ladyfingers in coffee, then layer the bottom of a glass baking dish with ladyfingers.
  4. Spread 1/2 of cream mixture evenly on top of the ladyfingers. Repeat process with the second layer of ladyfingers and cream mixture.
  5. Using a strainer, dust the top of your Tiramisu with cocoa powder. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  6. Slice and serve!
