Origin of The Tiramisu

The Tiramisu is a coffee flavoured Italian dessert. It is made of ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar and cheese,flavoured with cocoa. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.
